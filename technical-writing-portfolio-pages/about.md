      <h2>About Me</h2>
      <p>I am a technical writer with a foundation in software engineering and a strong interest in making complex information easier to use. My background in Java, Python, and web design helps me work comfortably with developers, understand technical systems, and create documentation that reflects real product behavior rather than surface-level descriptions.</p>
      <p>I write with a practical, audience-aware approach. That means organizing information by task, clarifying terminology, reducing ambiguity, and focusing on what users need in order to act. Whether the content is written for developers, support teams, internal stakeholders, or end users, my goal is the same: create documentation that is clear, useful, and trustworthy.</p>
    </section>

    <section class="resume-panel">
      <h2>How I Approach Documentation</h2>
      <ul class="resume-list">
        <li>Start with the user’s task, not the system’s internal complexity.</li>
        <li>Work closely with subject matter experts to validate technical accuracy.</li>
        <li>Structure content so readers can scan, understand, and complete work quickly.</li>
        <li>Use plain language, consistent terminology, and examples where they improve comprehension.</li>
        <li>Treat documentation as part of the product experience, not an afterthought.</li>
      </ul>
    </section>
